About US

Shiraaz is located at heart of Melbourne's city centre on busy William Street, where you can savour authentic tastes of India and be taken on an aromatic dining journey.
Shiraaz is an award winning brand that is one of the leading names in Indian Cuisine. Perfect for all occasions from corporate lunches to catching up with friends - Shiraaz can cater for any occasion.
Shiraaz brings diners an authentic Indian menu boasting an eclectic range of delicately spice infused temptations, right in the heart of Melbourne. The owners offer years of experience, operating highly successful Restaurants around Melbourne.
Shiraaz is fully licensed and offers a delightful wine list carefully selected to complement Indian cuisine; additionally there is a fabulous selection of beers and acolourful collection of cocktails to share with friends.
